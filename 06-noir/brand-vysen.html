VYSEN

Miami, independent. Japanese titanium, Italian acetate, one red mark on every pair.

One red mark, on every pair.

Vysen is an independent luxury eyewear house out of Miami, built on a single obsession: the temple-tip. Look for the signature red mark at the end of every arm — the quiet signal that a frame is Vysen, and nothing else. Each piece is engineered from Japanese titanium or hand-cut from Italian Mazzucchelli acetate, produced in limited runs rather than mass volume, so a Vysen frame stays rare by design. At Broadway Opticians, we carry the house for exactly that reason — the same restraint we bring to fitting a pair by hand.
