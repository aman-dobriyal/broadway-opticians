VYSEN

Miami, independent. The red temple-tip, now on the Mall Road.

One signature. No exceptions.

Vysen is an independent luxury house out of Miami, built on a single, uncompromising idea — a signature red temple-tip, set into every frame the House makes. Fronts are cut from Japanese titanium or Italian Mazzucchelli acetate, sourced for weight, colour depth and a fit that holds. Runs are kept deliberately limited: fewer frames, made properly, rather than more frames made faster. Nothing about Vysen apologises for the red. At Broadway Opticians we carry the full run currently available to India — one of the few boutiques outside the House's home market to do so.
