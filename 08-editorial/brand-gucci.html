GUCCI

Florence, 1921 — Mussoorie, today. The House's eyewear, kept the Broadway way.

A century, worn lightly.

Founded in Florence in 1921 by Guccio Gucci, the House began as a maker of fine leather goods and equestrian equipment — a heritage still visible in every green-red-green Web stripe. Under a century of Italian craftsmanship, Gucci grew into fashion's most recognisable name, its interlocking GG monogram a signature since 1933. Gucci eyewear, designed and produced by Kering Eyewear and made in Italy, carries these codes onto the face — bold acetates, gold-tone hardware, and silhouettes that move between quiet elegance and unapologetic maximalism. At Broadway Opticians, we are proud to bring authentic Gucci eyewear to Mussoorie.
