VYSEN

Miami · Titanium & Acetate · The Red Tip

Independent luxury eyewear, built without a fashion house behind it — and finished with a single red mark at the temple, on every frame.

The house
without a house.

Vysen is an independent luxury eyewear label out of Miami — no couture runway behind it, only the frame itself. Each piece is engineered in Japanese titanium and hand-polished Mazzucchelli acetate, built in limited runs rather than seasons. The house signs its work quietly: a single red bar set into the temple tip, present on every model, the way a jeweller stamps the inside of a band. Where the fashion houses sell a name, Vysen sells the object — architectural silhouettes, weightless hinges, and a collector's restraint in how few are made. At Broadway, we keep the Vysen drawer for guests who already know what they are looking at.
