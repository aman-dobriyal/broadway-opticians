The editorial

Made for the light up here.

Celine builds the CL 40293U the way the best hill houses were built — generously, in good material, with no interest in passing trends. The acetate is thick-milled and polished to a soft gloss; the silhouette is oversized without shouting about it; the gold-tone signature at the temple is the only jewellery it needs.

At 2,005 metres the light is different — thinner, sharper, quicker to flare off the snows. These lenses take that glare down to a murmur while keeping colour honest, so the view from Camel’s Back Road stays exactly as advertised.

Wear it on the promenade at four o’clock, when the mist starts thinking about coming up. It will still be right twenty years from now — which, on the Mall Road, is how we measure things.
