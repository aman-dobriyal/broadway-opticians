GUCCI

Florence, 1921 — Mussoorie, today. The House’s eyewear, kept the Broadway way.

A century, worn lightly.

Founded in Florence in 1921 by Guccio Gucci, the House began as a maker of fine leather goods and equestrian equipment — a heritage still visible in every green-red-green Web stripe and horsebit detail. Under a century of Italian craftsmanship, Gucci grew from a Florentine workshop into fashion’s most recognisable name, its interlocking GG monogram a signature since 1933. Gucci eyewear, crafted in Italy by Kering Eyewear, carries these codes onto the face: bold acetates, gold-tone hardware, the GG mark at the temple, and silhouettes that move between quiet elegance and unapologetic maximalism. At Broadway Opticians, we are proud to bring authentic Gucci eyewear to Mussoorie.
