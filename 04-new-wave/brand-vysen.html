Vysen

From ancient English — meaning "unique"

The Brand —

Some houses inherit
their name. Vysen chose theirs.

Drawn from ancient English, it means "unique." Founded in Miami in 2014 by two young entrepreneurs who traded fine jewelry for fine optics, VYSEN channels the city's cosmopolitan energy into eyewear that is anything but ordinary.

Each frame is handmade in Italy through forty-two meticulous steps, layering premium acetates and titanium into deconstructed, sculptural silhouettes finished with Carl Zeiss lenses. Look closely and you will find the house signature: a single red marking at the temple, a quiet footprint of passion on every pair. We are proud to bring these wearable pieces of art to Mussoorie — for those who see differently.

File Under: Unique

  • Founded in 2014 in Miami, Florida, by two entrepreneurs with a background in fine-jewelry marketing.
  • Handmade in Italy through 42 distinct production steps — premium acetates, stainless steel and titanium, Carl Zeiss lenses.
  • Every pair carries the signature red temple marking: the brand's "footprint," symbolizing passion and uniqueness.
  • Known for deconstructed, layered designs — Titanium, Rimless Titanium, Skeleton and Shield lines, with persona-named collections such as Dave, The Bill, Zavit, Thone and Seph.
  • The identity is strictly black-and-white minimalism. The product provides the color.
