The Brand

Some houses inherit their name. Vysen chose theirs.

Drawn from ancient English, it means "unique." Founded in Miami in 2014 by two young entrepreneurs who traded fine jewelry for fine optics, VYSEN channels the city's cosmopolitan energy into eyewear that is anything but ordinary.

Each frame is handmade in Italy through forty-two meticulous steps, layering premium acetates and titanium into deconstructed, sculptural silhouettes finished with Carl Zeiss lenses. Look closely and you will find the house signature: a single red marking at the temple, a quiet footprint of passion on every pair. We are proud to bring these wearable pieces of art to Mussoorie — for those who see differently.
