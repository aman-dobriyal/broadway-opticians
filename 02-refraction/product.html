The Editorial

Not worn. Exhibited.

Kuboraum mills each Maske from a single block of acetate, then cuts the mask-like silhouette by hand in Italy — no two pairs shade exactly alike. The P8 is a wraparound visor of a frame, built for faces that don't want to disappear into a crowd.

On the Mall Road it reads like a piece from the room it's named for: unapologetically sculptural, best worn low on the nose under mountain light, with nothing else on your face competing for attention.

Authenticity
Every pair leaves the boutique with its original Kuboraum case, cloth and certificate. Broadway Opticians is an authorized retailer — no grey market, ever.
Care
Clean with the cloth provided, never a sleeve. Store in the case when not on your face. Lifetime adjustments are on the house — walk in whenever the fit drifts.
