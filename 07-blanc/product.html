The editorial

A quiet kind of confidence

The CL 40293U is Celine at its most disciplined — an oversized cat-eye acetate that trades embellishment for proportion. The line at the brow, the taper at the hinge, the grey gradient lens that reads more Parisian dusk than mountain noon: this is a frame built for the long view, not the loud one.

At altitude, where the light comes at you from every direction — off the snow, off the mist, off the tin roofs on the Mall — the grey gradient earns its keep. We fit it the same way we fit everything at Broadway: by hand, adjusted to your face, with a Celine case and cloth in the bag.
